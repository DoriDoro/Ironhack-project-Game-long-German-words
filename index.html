Introduction

Please use the full screen of your computer to make all the words visible!
You will see in little boxes the German name including the title of a difficult and long word, right next to the German word there is the English translation.

The task is to learn how to write the German name including the title of the displayed words during 20 seconds and remember it.

After 20 seconds you have to write the German word in the textfield below the German name box. For this task you have 20 seconds of time.

The special characters in German language you find them in the little box under the textfield where you write down your answer.

Good luck!
